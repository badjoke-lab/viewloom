ABOUT THE OBSERVATORY

A narrow tool, built around distinct questions.

ViewLoom does not claim complete platform coverage. It records a bounded observed field, marks limitations, and gives each kind of movement its own view.

Why the views stay separate

A current snapshot and a thirty-day trend do not answer the same question. ViewLoom keeps them apart so scale, share, rivalry, and history are not collapsed into one generic dashboard.

What the numbers mean

Viewer counts are public observed values collected on a schedule. Viewer-minutes are derived from repeated snapshots and sample intervals. They are not official platform analytics and should not be read as exact creator revenue, unique viewers, or complete platform coverage.

Coverage and missing data

Each provider has its own observed set, collection behavior, retention window, and limitations. A fresh empty result is different from a collector failure. Partial, stale, demo, and missing states are shown explicitly. Twitch and Kick remain separate in routes, storage, rankings, exports, and analytical claims.

Independence

ViewLoom is an independent, unofficial project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by Twitch or Kick.

Questions and corrections

Use the Contact page for bug reports, data questions, payment-support questions, or requests about public information. Platform account and moderation issues should be directed to the relevant platform.
