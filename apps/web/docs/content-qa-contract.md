# About and Support QA Contract

This page records the current production contract for the About and Support pages.

- About and Support pages must keep the current dark shell and `data-provider="portal"`.
- Both pages must keep the global navigation, masthead, footer, contact link, GitHub link, and analytics entry.
- About must keep `.prose` content explaining separation of views, observed numbers, coverage/missing data, and independence.
- Support must keep `.support-options`, the secure Stripe payment link, policy copy, and the statement that support does not influence rankings or coverage.
- The footer contact form link must remain available on both pages.
- A future change that restores mock labels, fake status claims, or removes the independence/support limitation copy is a regression.
