ViewLoom currently accepts messages through an external Google Form. Opening the form leaves ViewLoom and is subject to Google's service and privacy terms.

Open contact formRead Privacy

What to include

Site bug

Route, provider, UTC time, browser/device, expected behavior, and what happened instead.

Data question

Provider, channel or page, date/time range, metric, and the exact value or state you are asking about.

Payment support

Payment date and amount, plus a non-sensitive receipt or payment reference if available. Never send complete card numbers, card security codes, passwords, or other payment credentials.

Public information request

Identify the page and the information you believe is incorrect or requires clarification, with supporting evidence where available.

Operator disclosure requests

Requests for operator name, address, or telephone information associated with the current support-payment flow may be submitted through the contact form. Where disclosure is required, the information will be provided without delay before the transaction.

Do not use ViewLoom contact channels for Twitch or Kick account access, moderation appeals, bans, payouts, subscriptions, creator verification, or other platform-support matters. Contact the relevant platform directly.

Response boundary

ViewLoom is independently operated and does not promise a specific response time. Messages may be prioritized by security impact, payment urgency, reproducibility, and whether the issue concerns ViewLoom itself.
